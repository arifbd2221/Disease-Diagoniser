Welcome to Doctorola.com ! By signing up with Doctorola.com, you confirm your understanding and agreement that each appointment brought to you by Doctorola.com will have a nominal fee of BDT 20. Please see the details here.

Doctorola.com is an online appointment marketplace that promotes the registered Doctors in different platforms and acquires patient appointments for them by speciality and location. Doctorola.com also verifies and confirms each appointment through intense engagement with the patients. For bringing each appointment, Doctorola will have a nominal fee charged to the Doctor. The amount will be payable end of each month combining for all your Doctorola appointments fulfilled in the month. There will be no fee charged for appointments acquired by the Doctor personally or through any other source. Doctorola.com will also not charge for any appointment missed due to patients' attendance.

For details or clarification, please contact us at contact@doctorola.com
